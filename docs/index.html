Know where you stand with your AI coding tools. Kerstel reads local credentials and shows your usage at a glance.

CLI

kerstel open # launch the app
kerstel stop # stop the app
kerstel restart # restart
kerstel update # pull latest & rebuild
kerstel version # show version
kerstel uninstall # remove everything

Requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later and Swift (included with Xcode Command Line Tools). Launches from Spotlight after install.
